QuickBooks ODOO Connector

Integration of QuickBooks with ODOO

Overview

ODOO is a fully integrated suite of business modules that encompass the traditional ERP functionality. QuickBooks is geared toward small and medium-sized businesses and offer on-premises accounting applications that accept business payments, manage and pay bills, and payroll functions. ODOO integration with QuickBooks give us power of managing QuickBooks data from ODOO.

Key Features And Functions

  • Export/Update ODOO's customer to QuickBooks
  • Export/Update ODOO's products to QuickBooks
  • Export/Update ODOO's invoices to QuickBooks
  • Import/Update QuickBooks's customer in ODOO
  • Import/Update QuickBooks's products in ODOO
  • Import/Update QuickBooks's invoices in ODOO
  • Auto Synchronization for Export/Update ODOO's customer to QuickBooks
  • Auto Synchronization for Export/Update ODOO's products to QuickBooks
  • Auto Synchronization for Export/Update ODOO's invoices to QuickBooks
  • Auto Synchronization for Import/Update QuickBooks's customer in ODOO
  • Auto Synchronization for Import/Update QuickBooks's products in ODOO
  • Auto Synchronization for Import/Update QuickBooks's invoices in ODOO

Sync ODOO Customers with QuickBooks

  • Go to Sales --> Customers
  • In tree view select customers you want to export to QuickBooks
  • Go to actions and click Sync to QuickBooks

Sync ODOO Products with QuickBooks

  • Go to Sales --> Products
  • In tree view select products you want to export to QuickBooks
  • Go to actions and click Sync to QuickBooks

Sync ODOO Invoices with QuickBooks

  • Go to Invoicing --> Customer Invoices
  • In tree view select invoices you want to export to QuickBooks
  • Go to actions and click Sync to QuickBooks

Import QuickBooks Customers, Products, Invoices in ODOO

  • Go to QuickBoooks from Apps
  • Select options from customers, products, invoices that you want to import.
  • Click "SYNC" button. You will se message at the completion of sync.
  • If you see any error message. It would be due to access token expiration. Go to Settings-->user->QuickBooks Credentials and refresh token

Automated Import Actions Settings

  • Go to QuickBooks--> QuickBooks Auto Settings--> Import Settings
  • Here you can see settings for customers, products and invoices auto-import
  • select the checkbox to activate sync
  • Also select sync interval number and interval unit and save settings.
  • This may take some time if scheduler is already running because you cannot update a scheduler if it is running. It will be updated after it finishes it's task.

Automated Import Actions Settings

  • Go to QuickBooks--> QuickBooks Auto Settings--> Export Settings
  • Here you can see settings for customers, products and invoices auto-import
  • select the checkbox to activate sync
  • Also select sync interval number and interval unit and save settings.
  • This may take some time if scheduler is already running because you cannot update a scheduler if it is running. It will be updated after it finishes it's task.

Synced Data on QuickBooks

  • Go to https://sandbox.qbo.intuit.com/app/sales
  • Here you can see customers, products and invoices that you exported from ODOO
