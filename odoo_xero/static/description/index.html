Odoo-Xero Connector

ODOO is a fully integrated suite of business modules that encompass the traditional ERP functionality. Xero is the QuickBooks alternative. Xero is an accounting software to manage invoicing, bank reconciliation, bookkeeping & more. Odoo integration with Xero gives us the way to sync your all Odoo's Products, Taxes, Account of Charts, Bills and Invoices with Xero to enjoy its services.



Demo Video





Key Features And Functions

  • Import Products from Xero.
  • Export single or multiple products to Xero.
  • Import Contacts from Xero.
  • Export single or multiple Contacts to Xero.
  • Import all types of Invoices and Bills from Xero.
  • Export single or multiple Invoices and Bills to Xero.
  • Export and Imports Tax Rates from xero with Intial Sync.
  • Export and Imports Chart of Accounts to Xero with Intial Sync.
  • Export and Import Currency with Invoice Syncing.


Xero Credentials

  • Predefined Xero Public App Credentials Setup.
  • You just need to Get Code after 30 min and then Authenticate that code.


Exporting and Importing Contacts




  • Export Single or Multiple Contacts in Odoo to your Xero Organization
  • Import All Contacts from xero.


Exporting and Importing Products




  • Export Single or Multiple Products in Odoo to your Xero Organization.
  • Import All products from xero.


Exporting and Importing Invoices and Bills




  • Export Single or Multiple Invoices and Bills from Odoo to your Xero Organization
  • Import Invoices and Bills from Xero


Requirements




  • Download PyXero Python Module from Link
  • Install the module from setup.py file.
  • OR Run "pip install pyxero== 0.9.1" command in the Terminal.
