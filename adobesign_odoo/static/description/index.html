Adobe Sign-ODOO

With Adobe Sign, agencies of all types can cut the cost and hassle of paper-based, manual tasks — while gaining more control and visibility over document workflows. Odoo is a fully integrated suite of business modules that encompass the traditional ERP functionality. Adobe Sign in Adobe Document Cloud provides trusted electronic and digital signature solutions that allow you to run business processes. Integration of Adobe Sign with ODOO provides us the power of Adobe Sign signature within ODOO.

Key Features And Functions

  • Directly send document to adobe sign from odoo.
  • No need for an adobe sign application. Just an adobe sign account is enough.
  • Check agreement status from odoo.
  • Agent role for multiple customers using odoo.
  • Support for both Enterprise and Community odoo version.


Entering AdobeSign Credentials in your odoo

  • Go to AdobeSign Setting and click create and add credentials.
  • Enter Your AdobeSign account Name.
  • Copy client id and client secret from AdobeSign developers app and past it in odoo.
  • Also enter redirect URL.

Send pdf files for eSignature

  • Send Odoo pdf files for signature via email for user.
  • Send Multiple attachments.


Check Agreement's Recipient Status

  • Management of Agreement’s recipient status on Odoo.
